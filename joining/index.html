Research Foci:

We are interested in the cognitive mechanisms that allow people to flexibly communicate, collaborate, and coordinate with one another in social interactions. Our work includes computational models of human cognition, large-scale web experiments, and neuroimaging studies.

Minimum Requirements for Undergraduate Research Assistants:

  • 3.5 GPA preferred
  • coursework in Cognitive Psychology, Language Science, and/or Computer Science

How to Apply:

Send your:

  • resume/CV
  • unofficial transcript
  • a brief letter of interest

to Prof. Robert Hawkins (rdhawkins@wisc.edu) and Yuliya Zubak (yzubak@wisc.edu)
